College Tuition Costs are Rising

So I wrote this paper for my comp class and it was for one of our five projects. The next project of the term was to remix (to combine or edit existing materials to produce something new) one of our previous projects and so I chose to take the information from that paper and present it in a website format along with having this website just have stuff about me and stuff. So yeah. :P If you would like to view my paper just click on it.

So at this point of my website you probably saw all of these words and you got bored instantly. You know that feeling you get when you look at a page full of boring text (like chemistry, I mean come on, who likes chemistry?) and you just realize you can't even? Well the solution then, is obviously to spice things up a bit! You've gotta take that boring stuff and make it cool and interesting! So here's the facts about college tuition. (but spicier!)

  • Fact: College tuition has risen 5.4% faster than inflation for the last 10 years and this trend continues.
  • Fact: Real net average tuition (tuition after grants and other aid) rose 33.1% over the last decade.
  • Fact: A large reason for tuition increases is a cut in public funding. Public funding has decreased 21% on a per-pupil basis since the year 2000.
  • Fact: 55% of students receive financial aid just to have the ability to achieve post-secondary education.
